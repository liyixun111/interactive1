Red Alert

Red Alert 2 is the very first game I played in my life. It was very popular during my elementary school among the boys. Since it is a game that you are the commander and you organize every soldieries and buildings, me and my friends usually talked about the strategies and played against with each other.

Might & Magic Heroes

This game was struggled me for a while. I found this game in my cousin’s computer, and he told me it was good for my intelligence… I agree. I tried to play a lot of times, it seems like my intelligence was not there yet, and it was also a childhood pain of mine. Really hard…

Warcraft III

This is one of my favorite and almost every boy born in 90s are familiar with this game because the game DOTA1 was very popular, and Chinese DOTA team won a lot of competition around the world, and DOTA1 was based on this game, meaning all the characters and maps come from the Warcraft III. There are four factions in the game: Humans, Orcs, Elves, and the Undead. They all have strong and weaknesses, depends on the duration of time. The Undead is my favorite, and I still recognize some short cut for building elements.

Counter-Strike

To me, CS and Warcraft 3 are in the same level because there was a television channel mainly playing these two games when I was young, and I was always fighting with my grandmother for the remote control. I first played this game in my older cousin’s computer, and he taught me how to use some of the guns in the game to be more accurate.

Diablo

Diablo is very fun to play, but I always play offline. The most thing I really enjoyed was the various and random attribute on the weapons and equipments and they all depends on you luckiness. And there are different characters in the game you can choose. I also like Undead in this game too because they can summon skeleton soldiers help you.

King Of Kings

I have never played this game before, but it seems like the very first online game in China. Maybe I just enjoyed too much with Warcraft 3.

World of Warcraft

WOW was the most popular online game before the League of Legends. But I didn’t play this game when I was young, I played this during my high school time. 8 guys in my dormitory played together every night after class. Sometimes with other dormitory and also girl’s dormitory too… Very crazy and easy to be addicted to this game because there are sooooo many fun things you can do in this game. I was addicted to archaeology because you can find a lot of things with finding the treasure. So rich students were addicted to collecting mounts, dragons are cool to ride…
